# WOK!N — Instagram Content Calendar

> **Goal:** Build a wildly engaging, share-worthy IG presence. No boring plate shots. No generic "food porn." We're building a *personality*, not a portfolio.

---

## THE BIG IDEA — Brand Voice & Content Pillars

**Voice:** Bold, slightly chaotic, fast-paced, self-aware, never takes itself too seriously. Think *Wendy's Twitter meets a Bangkok street kitchen*.

**Tagline hooks to weaponize:**
- "From our wok to your table"
- "Bold flavours. Zero compromises."
- "Catch you on your next flavour trip"

### 5 Content Pillars (rotate daily)

| # | Pillar | What It Is | Why It Goes Viral |
|---|--------|------------|-------------------|
| 1 | **WOK CAM** | POV/macro shots of actual wok action, fire, oil, sizzle | ASMR + visual ASMR = saves & shares |
| 2 | **CHEF UNHINGED** | Chef commentary, hot takes, kitchen drama, ranking food | Personality = followers |
| 3 | **CUSTOMER CHAOS** | Real customer reactions, dares, challenges, "first bite" | UGC fuel + relatability |
| 4 | **STREET SMART** | Pan Asian food facts, myth busting, "did you know" | Educational = saves |
| 5 | **MEME LORD** | Trending audio, restaurant memes, food humour | Reach + algorithm love |

## DAILY STORIES PLAYBOOK

Stories are where the cult is built. Post **3–5 per day minimum**. Mix:

### Daily Story Mix (rotate)
1. **Behind the Pass** — quick clip of kitchen action, no edit, raw
2. **Polls/Quizzes** — "spicy or saucy?", "noodles or rice?", dish vs dish
3. **Question Stickers** — "what should we cook tomorrow?", "rate today's vibe"
4. **Countdown** — to Heat Test, to weekend specials, to new menu drop
5. **UGC Reshare** — anyone who tags you, reshare with a fun overlay
6. **"This or That"** — daily battle template
7. **Sneak Peek** — tomorrow's reel, blurred or 2-second teaser

### Story Templates to Build Once, Reuse Forever
- **"Wokin Wrapped"** weekly — top dish ordered, busiest hour, weirdest order
- **"Spice Meter Monday"** — vote how spicy your week feels
- **"Friday Flavour Forecast"** — what's selling out this weekend
- **"Sunday Sauce Talk"** — chef opens up about a dish, vulnerable
- **"Throwback Thali"** — old menu items, ask if they should return

## VIRAL MECHANICS — The Tactical Playbook

### Hooks That Stop Thumbs (use in first 1.5 seconds)
- "POV: you walked into Wokin and..."
- "Things I'd never tell my customers but..."
- "Tell me you eat at Wokin without telling me..."
- "I tried [trend] using a wok and..."
- "This is illegal in [X] country but we do it anyway..."
- "Stop scrolling. You need to know this about [dish]."
- "Chef said don't post this but..."

### Caption Formulas
1. **Hot take + emoji combo** — "Pineapple belongs on noodles. Fight me. 🍍🍜"
2. **Question hook** — "Be honest: when's the last time you slurped noodles like you meant it?"
3. **Tag bait** — "Tag the friend who orders the SAME dish every single time."
4. **Save bait** — "Bookmark this for your next date night order."
5. **Controversy bait** — "Ranking our dishes worst to best. Don't unfollow us."

### Posting Times (Pakistan)
- **Reels:** 1 PM (lunch scroll), 8 PM (dinner scroll), 11 PM (bored scroll)
- **Stories:** Throughout the day, peaks at 12 PM, 6 PM, 10 PM
- **Carousels:** Sundays at 11 AM (long-form attention day)

### Hashtag Strategy
- 3 brand: #WokinKarachi #BoldFlavours #WokinFamily
- 5 niche: #PakistanFoodie #KarachiEats #PanAsianFood #SpicyFood #FoodieGram
- 2 trending: monitor weekly, swap in
- **NEVER 30 hashtags. 8–12 max.**

## CONTENT TO AVOID (Anti-Patterns)

❌ Static plate photos with no story
❌ "Open from 11AM - 11PM!" announcements as a feed post
❌ Overly cinematic 4-min food films (boring, low save rate)
❌ Generic "happy customers" testimonials with stock-feel music
❌ Long captions explaining the dish in detail (nobody reads)
❌ Reposting the same dish 4 weeks in a row
❌ Reels longer than 30 seconds unless it's a story format

## "OFF-MENU" SECRET WEAPON

Create 1–2 dishes that are **only orderable if you mention an Instagram code word**. Different code each month. Builds:
- Follower loyalty (must follow to know)
- Reason to engage every post (might miss the code)
- UGC explosion (people film themselves ordering it)
- Word-of-mouth ("you HAVE to follow them on IG")

Example launch reel: *"There's a dish on our menu you've never seen. Only one way to order it. 👀 Follow for the code."*
